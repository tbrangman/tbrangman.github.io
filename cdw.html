m.cdw.com

CDW Corporation is a Fortune 500 company that provides technology products and services for businesses, governments, and educational institutions.

First contact

I was able to work with CDW Corporation in the summer of 2016 as they were gearing toward a revamped mobile website after years of using the same interface. The company would be completing their first rounds of rebranding on web and mobile, moving toward a more modern design.

My role

During my internship, my colleague (another college student) and I was tasked with the implementation of phase one for their new mobile website rebranding. Phase one included: smoother product search, autocomplete search, and updated UI development. I worked closely with the UX team to understand their vision for the new rebranding and assist in turning their vision into a functional experience online. I was able to take part in design sprints and provide my knowledge as a web developer to determine the feasibility and usefulness of certain features.

Technical solutions

The finished product included new UI elements, updated marketing content for the landing page, connection of the back-end warehouse storage system with the front-end UI, newly-designed menu, optimization, unit testing, and a product icon style guide for the Marketing team.
